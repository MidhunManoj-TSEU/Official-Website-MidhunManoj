About Me

Midhun Manoj is the Founder & CEO of TechSparks IT Training Institute, an educational initiative dedicated to providing free beginner-friendly programming and GATE coaching content in Malayalam. With a strong passion for empowering students through accessible and high-quality education, he actively leads content creation across YouTube and social media platforms.

He is the creator of two popular YouTube channels: TechSparks (focused on IT and programming tutorials) and TechSparks GATE Coaching Institute (for GATE exam preparation).

He is a certified Data Science and Machine Learning professional from the Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago, and is currently pursuing his Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Haryana.

His mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world IT industry requirements by offering mock interviews, resume building, and skill-based training through TechSparks.
